About the school

A Legacy of Academic Excellence in Nigeria

For over 25 years, Greenfield Academy has been shaping young Nigerians into confident, articulate and morally grounded leaders. We blend a rigorous WAEC/NECO curriculum with co-curricular activities, spiritual formation and life skills.

Our students consistently top regional placements, win debate cups, and gain admission into Nigeria's top universities and respected institutions abroad.

Academic Programs

Junior & Senior Secondary Programmes

Aligned with the Nigerian National Curriculum and WAEC/NECO standards.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1–3)

A broad foundation that prepares students for the BECE.

  • English & Mathematics
  • Basic Science & Technology
  • Civic Education & Social Studies
  • Yoruba / Igbo / Hausa
  • Computer Studies

Senior Secondary — Sciences

For future doctors, engineers and tech leaders.

  • Mathematics & Further Maths
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Geography
  • Agricultural Science
  • Computer Science

Senior Secondary — Arts & Commercial

For future lawyers, entrepreneurs and creatives.

  • Literature in English
  • Government & History
  • Economics & Commerce
  • Financial Accounting
  • CRS / IRS
